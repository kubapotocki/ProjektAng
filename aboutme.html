Reading

I started reading because I'm fascinated by the opportunity to immerse myself in worlds created by words. Word by word, page by page, I discover unknown spaces filled with adventures, emotions, and wisdom. Reading opens the doors to new perspectives, nurtures my imagination, and teaches me about the world and myself. It's a path to endless knowledge and the unfathomable beauty of literature.

Taking Photos

I love taking photos because through the camera lens, I can capture fleeting moments and preserve them forever. By framing and capturing the most beautiful sights, events, and people, I create extraordinary mementos that last longer than just in my memory. Photos are like windows to the past that I can open at any moment and revisit those magical times. Through photography, I discover new perspectives, observe the world more attentively, and learn to appreciate the beauty in the smallest details.

Programming

I started programming in high school, and it immediately captivated me. The world of coding is incredibly fascinating, and it has the potential to greatly improve the future. Discovering the secrets of algorithms and creating functional programs brings me immense satisfaction. I firmly believe that programming skills will be essential in the future to drive innovation and enhance various fields such as medicine, transportation, and energy.
